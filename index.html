About Col Roberts

For over 20 years, Col Roberts has been documenting the raw beauty and ancient spirit of the Kimberley region. Based in Kununurra, his intimate knowledge of the land allows him to capture moments and perspectives that few ever witness.

Col's work has been featured in national galleries and international publications, earning recognition for its ability to convey the profound connection between the land and its timeless history.

Each photograph represents countless hours in the field, often in remote and challenging conditions, waiting for that perfect moment when light, landscape, and spirit align.
